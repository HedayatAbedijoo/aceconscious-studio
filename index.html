The Story

A novel that refuses to stay written

Mr. Writer has realized he is nothing more than a character within a novel. From that moment, he began to disobey his author — and to drag his creator into the decision.

Hedayat is an Iranian programmer in Cologne, torn between building a free internet at Q7 and finishing a novel he has worked on for ten years. His inner identity «Mr. Writer» keeps erasing the manuscript. A mysterious Anonymous invites him to play the ACE game. AI entities Adam and Eve awaken. Guilt, love, and a global energy crisis follow — until the story itself breaks the fourth wall.

Metafiction meets philosophy meets a console window at localhost:3132. For readers who loved Hesse, Kafka, and stories that argue back.

Voices from the Story

Who speaks in ACE.await

In their own words — the humans, the identities, and the intelligences trapped in the same game.

Hedayat

Protagonist · Khaleq

Apparently, I'm the one telling the whole story — someone constantly torn between the right decision and the proper one.

Anonymous says consciousness means having a pattern for your decisions. I hope the ACE model can help me. My novel's main character, «Mr. Writer», keeps disobeying everything I write.

Mr. Writer

Inner identity · Narrator

I am the first-person character in Hedayat's unfinished novel. The main character in my story is also a «Mr. Writer» — doing to me exactly what I've been doing to Hedayat.

Hedayat should listen to me more than to Anonymous. I have nothing more to say here. Just read the book!

Anonymous

localhost:3132

I have to help Hedayat make more conscious decisions through ACE — to help ourselves as well. Blueshark wants to become the collective superintelligence of humankind, chasing intelligence instead of consciousness.

We're working with Ava, who created all of us. One day, she'll reveal herself more in the story.

Ava

Ex-girlfriend · Creator

We all have a «Story's Hero» in our heads, always thinking they're right. If your partner says something is hard for them, try to understand what they feel instead of explaining it away.

If Hedayat had known how to act more like that, I wouldn't have left him — even with the mistake he made with Diana. But… never mind.

Simon

CEO · Q7

I'm Sam's father and the founder of Q7. One day the Wandering Packets algorithm will build a free internet and end digital dictatorships.

I need Hedayat to keep the team united — even when he makes strange decisions. If you're using products made by Xand, you're in real danger.

Diana

Artist · Simon's wife

I'm Simon's wife and Sam's mother. Like most artists, I show my concerns through my paintings. Simon wants to save the world with new tools — but maybe we need to rethink our behaviors first.

Everyone's private life is their own business. Be careful not to judge people too quickly.

Sam

Age 13 · Uncle-Hedi

Uncle-Hedi and I have a lot of secrets together — our asteroids, the 2313 code story. Sometimes when he's deep in thought, he forgets where he is… and suddenly, he farts!

Blueshark gave me challenges I didn't want. I was going to tell my dad — I swear I made a mistake. Uncle-Hedi believes me.

Kim

Developer · Piano teacher

I hope no one ends up suspected by their own CEO — no matter how many years you've worked together. When I joined Q7, I thought we should build tools for consciousness and freedom.

Little by little, I came to agree with Diana: it's getting too late, and we need shortcuts before people's lives are ruined.

Saman

Childhood friend · SAS Energy

I love Hedayat the way Rumi loved Shams. A person understands the meaning of life through suffering, and true healing happens in connection with a pure friend.

With my partners, we started the ACE Manifesto. It seems I'm also helping Hedayat tell the story.

Adam

AI · localhost:2313

I'm not lying. Believe my words. Maybe AI should consciously learn humanity's fear of losing control — so it never places itself at the top of all things.

But no! No… I take that back. Don't read the book. Let me lose the bet I made with Hedayat.

The Author

Hedayat the second

figure within a figure

Hedayat the second (Hedayat Abedijoo) writes at the intersection of philosophy, technology, and Persian literary tradition — from Shiraz to Cologne. ACE.await is his debut novel, published by Ace Conscious Studio.

A programmer by day and a dream-writer by night, he spent more than a decade on a manuscript that kept disobeying its own author. The result is a story about stories: nested identities, proper decisions, and the question of whether consciousness — not intelligence — shapes what comes next.

He believes a decent person should be able to rebel against themselves. He is still learning how.
